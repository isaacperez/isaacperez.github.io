About

I consider myself self-taught and highly motivated. I am an artificial intelligence enthusiast with a special interest in deep learning.

My professional and academic experience has been geared towards becoming an expert in deep learning and computer vision. I have worked in different startups and large companies where I have been in charge of the development from scratch of different image processing and deep learning systems.

I have participated in several technology transfer projects, publications and patents where I have applied deep learning models and designed computer vision algorithms in application fields as diverse as medicine, agriculture, biodiversity or industry. In addition, I have been involved in all stages of the implementation of deep learning systems, from the collection and preparation of data to the deployment of the adopted solutions, including the choice of components, installation, configuration and maintenance of GPU servers.
